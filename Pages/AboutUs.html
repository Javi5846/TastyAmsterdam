ABOUT US

I could say the story begins at the beggining of the '90s, when my mother asked me "Do you want to eat something?" And my answer was: "No ma, I already had a snack" of course my mother needed to check because it was incredible, I was probably 3 or 4 years old, but yes ... I had cooked something myself and also had done the dishes. So I can say that I started cooking in my early years. With the time I started to pay attention when an experienced person was cooking, like grandma or a cook. I've learnt tips from different cultures, ages, countries, etc. cooking around the world. Looking forward to improve my skills all the time.

Always as fresh as possible !!! Buying in the best markets in town !!!
