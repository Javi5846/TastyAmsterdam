Is our food Gluten Free?

GLUTEN are proteins found in cereals such as wheat, spelled and rye. Our food COULD contains gluten, including spelled bread. Gluten is digested and absorbed by the body, just like other proteins. However, some people are allergic or intolerant to gluten, these people cannot eat some of our food.
